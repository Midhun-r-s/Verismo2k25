RULES AND REGULATIONS
  1. Team should be of 2 persons.
  2. The team with the most cumulative points from all rounds will be declared as the winner.
  3. There will be a strict time limit for each event.
  4. The team must assemble at 10.15am itself.
  5. College ID card is mandatory for participation.
  6. The 2 Teams with least scores will be eliminated at the end of each round.
  7. Organizers reserve every right to add or remove any rules as they fit.
  8. College ID card is mandatory for participation.
  9. Registration Fee: 200
